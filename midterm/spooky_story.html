The Black Snake


Jasper was obsessed with Little Big Snake, but one night, he encountered something terrifying

massive black snake with no name tag that stalked his every move. No matter how fast he slithered,

it was always there, closer and closer.


Suddenly, the chat flashed an impossible message:

“You’re part of the game now.”


Panicking, Jasper tried to quit, but the game froze. His screen flickered, revealing his own terrified

face trapped in the black snake’s scales. As the snake lunged, he felt a crushing force wrap around

his chest, squeezing tighter and tighter.


The game went dark with one final message:

“Welcome forever.”


Now, other players report a strange black snake, silent and nameless, lurking in their games. Jasper?

Gone.
